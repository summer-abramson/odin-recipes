Pookie's Tuscan Chicken

Description

This is what we cooked the first time I ever stayed at your home. It was so freaking good.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Cherry Tomatos
  • Cream Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Spinach
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Prep chicken, cut breast in half
  2. Prep filling, chop cherry tomatos, chop spinach, and mix with cream cheese
  3. Stuff chicken with filling, and put in oven at 300 degrees
  4. Begin reducing cream and tomatos in sauce pan
  5. Plate chicken and top with sauce
