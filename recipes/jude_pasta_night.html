Jude's Pasta Night

Description

This is a super simple, but yummy pasta dish that can be made with ingridents from the Georgia Tech Dining Hall. This is a great refresh from the grease of North Avenue.

Ingredients

  • Spagetti
  • Cherry Tomatos
  • Parmasean
  • Olive Oil
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Begin by boiling pasta for suggested time on package
  2. Next stew tomatos, in olive oil, till thick and creamy
  3. Add spinach to sauce pan
  4. Once pasta is done, drain water, and add paramsean and stir
  5. Add pasta to plate, and top with sauce, and more parmasean
